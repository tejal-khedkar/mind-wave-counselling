My Methodology

How I Actually Work With You

No one-size-fits-all scripts here. I draw on three evidence-based approaches and blend them to fit your life, your brain, and your goals.

01

Rational Emotive Behaviour Therapy (REBT)

Spot the thought spirals that fuel your stress — and replace them with thinking that's actually useful. Less "I'm failing at life," more "this is hard, and I can handle it."

02

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

Understand how your thoughts, feelings, and actions feed each other — then break the cycles that aren't working for you.

03

Integrative Therapy

Sometimes one approach isn't enough. I draw from multiple models to meet you where you actually are — not where a textbook says you should be.

What to expect in a session

  • No judgement — you bring whatever's on your mind
  • You set the pace; nothing is forced
  • Practical tools you can use between sessions
  • First session is simply a conversation

This is for you if…

  • You feel stuck but can't quite explain why
  • You're high-functioning but quietly exhausted
  • You want to understand yourself, not just cope
